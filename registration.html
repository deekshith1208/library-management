Facilities for Library Members:

  1. Access to Digital library.
  2. Can extend borrowing time through digital account.
  3. Access to WIFI facility in the library.
  4. Get notification on library events.
  5. Can use at food court for later payments(limited).

Application process:

  1. Register your detail on our website.
  2. Our staff will contact you for further steps.
  3. After creating membership account secret barcode will send to you.
  4. You can get E-card at our library.

Notes:

*You will get a notice when your E-card is about to expire.To renew your account, please contact the library in person, by phone, or online.

*If you lose your E-library card, please contact us as soon as possible. Library staff can stop your card until it can be found or replaced. You are responsible for all materials checked out on your library card until its loss is reported.
